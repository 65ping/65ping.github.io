Running an Online Survey

User Research excerpt - Elixir Berlin

Elixir Berlin is a non profit organisation that facilitates and organises Elixir programming monthly meetings in Berlin. Therefore, continuation of the group activities depends on donations and volunteer acts. Since the beginning Elixir Berlin has tried to evaluate their work quality to keep their users happy and satisfied to drive more users into their group and platform.
However, with the recent changes of social platforms, COVID-19 pandemic and also growing needs of their customers and users they have decided to organise their meetings using their own platform (Junto) as they have a big number of active users. As a result, I was assigned to improve their platform and assist them to make the changes as seamless as possible for their users. 
After searching through their history and conducting an ideation workshop with the stockholders to clarify the needs and goals, I came to the conclusion to run an online survey. Therefore, I designed a set of questionnaire and ran the survey using Google forms.

My role
  • Facilitating online ideation session
  • Designing questionnaire
  • Analysing the survey's results
  • UI design
Tools
  • Miro
  • Google forms
  • trello
  • Sketchapp
  • Zepline

Challenge

  • Short time span
  • Large number of users needed to participate to the survey
  • Running online ideation workshop
  • Analysing the survey result to take the right action and plan the overall journey of the project
