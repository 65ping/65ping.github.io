Dunnhumby Germany GmbH - Creative Services Team

Website design

Creative services team was a diverse group of designers part of Sociomantic Labs GmbH, a Berlin based global advertising firm that was founded in 2009. Sociomantic used real-time bidding technology to purchase and display personalised digital advertising in milliseconds and it was operating in 60+ markets and 19 offices worldwide. In 2014 Sociomantic Lab was acquired by dunnhumby ( a global customer data science company subsidiary of Tesco).
Since then Sociomantic was transformed to dunnhumby Germany GmbH. As a young and small entity part of a big group (more than 3000 employees), Creative team services requested to design a website that could showcase its capabilities and experiences to the rest of dunnhumby group.
